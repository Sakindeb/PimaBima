Make Deposits

  • Cash Deposits via Lipa na M-PESA:
    • Go to the Lipa na M-PESA menu on your mobile phone.
    • Select "Pay Bill" option.
    • Enter the business number: 123456.
    • Enter your account number as the account number field.
    • Enter the amount you wish to deposit.
    • Enter your M-PESA PIN and confirm the transaction.
    • Keep the transaction confirmation message for reference.
  • BongaPoints Transfer:
    • Go to the BongaPoints menu on your mobile phone.
    • Select "Transfer" option.
    • Enter the recipient's phone number.
    • Enter the amount of BongaPoints you wish to transfer.
    • Review the details and confirm the transfer.
    • Keep the transaction confirmation message for reference.

Lipa na M-PESA
